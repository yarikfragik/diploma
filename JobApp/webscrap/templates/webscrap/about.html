About

"Find Job" is an online Job Searching Web application , web application through which job seekers can register and apply for jobs. A job seeker must find jobs through advertisements, college fairs, job fairs, etc., and employers must put in much effort to find the right candidate for a vacant position. This application addresses such shortcomings and is a convenient platform for job seekers to find and apply for jobs. Candidates can search for jobs in any field through advanced search techniques. Our scraper scraps the data from the job portal and then provides job options to the user or job seeker according to their needs such as location, salary, working time, technical skills, etc. They can upload their basic information related to job search which is stored in a database. Candidates can use this portal without any geographical barriers, from any part of the world. Also, our portal will notify the registered users through email notifications consisting of basic information about jobs and the application link for that job.
